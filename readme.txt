=========================GAMESTATES=========================
    State 1: Splash

          Press the 'any' key at this screen to begin game

    State 2: Play

          You are the amazing Oscillating Avian(*TM)
              and you must jump fly over green pipes
              to escape from evil Cats!

          Be  careful not to hit the pipes or fly too
              low or too high!

    State 3: Game Over

          You were captured! Press SELECT / BKSPACE
              to escape and try again!

==========================CONTROLS==========================

    SELECT ----> Go to the game's "Splash" screen at
                    any time

    START ----> Immediate Game Over, get captured by
                    Cats!

    Hold or tap:
    A, B, UP,   ---->  Flap your wings to gain height!
    DOWN, L, R
